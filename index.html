Description

Plant diseases and pest infestation are major issues faced by farmers every year. Pests and Diseases result in the destruction of crops or part of the plant resulting in decreased food production leading to food insecurity. Understanding the disease and the development process is the first step towards successful disease management. To solve this problem comprehensively, we have made this web app so that anyone could easily find out the specific plant disease and get its cure fast. The dataset that we have used was collected via Kaggle API, the model was built using Keras MobileNet and finally deployed the model using tensorflow.js. Hopefully, this will help people a lot.
